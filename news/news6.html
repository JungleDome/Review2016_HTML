UK voted to leave EU (European Union)

The people of UK has voted to leave the European Union (EU) in a historic referendum that forced the country’s prime minister, David Cameron to step down as he forcefully campaigned to stay in the EU. The electoral officials said that the “Leave” campaign had racked up 17.4 million votes compared to 16.1 million backing the status quo.
The eu is already grappling with a refugee crisis and persistent economic woes. The “Remain” campaigners had argued that a Brexit would be a danger for the British people and even global economy. However, the “Leave” campaigners were celebrating their triumph as the result of this voting has fully satisfied their needs.
