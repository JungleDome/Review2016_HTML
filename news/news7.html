The fall of South Korea President, Park Geun-hye

The president of South Korea, Park Geun-hye has been forced to step down as she has been found responsible over a corruption scandal. The president has pressured large companies into donating to an organization. Her longtime friend, Choi Soon-sil was involved in running that organization which is nonprofitable called K-Sports. The state prosecutors launch a special investigation team to have a deep look the scandal. They believed Park aided Choi by alleged bullying companies into giving tens of millions of dollars to organization and business Choi controlled.
Lawmakers had passed the impeachment bill on her by a vote of 234 for and 56 opposed. The Constitutional Court begins preparations for Park’s trial. In addition, the billionaire scion of Samsung, who is Lee Jae-yong has been arrested over the suspicions that he bribed Park and Choi in exchange for better business favors. As the final court, the members of the Constitutional Court votes unanimously to remove Park from office.
