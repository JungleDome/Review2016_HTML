Zika virus outbreak in Asia

“The World Health Organization has said it is highly likely that infections and outbreak of Zika will continue to rise in Asia.” According to the WHO, there are at least 19 countries have reported Zika virus locally transmitted.
Thailand and Singapore has recorded 350 to 400 cases including pregnant women. How Zika Virus spread from Africa? Zika virus transmitted through mosquito bite, from mother to son., through sex and through blood transfusion.
