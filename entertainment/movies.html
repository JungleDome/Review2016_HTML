TOP 10 BEST MOVIES TO WATCH IN 2016

TOP 1
Deadpool

Actors: Ryan Reynolds(Deadpool), Morena Baccarin(Copycat), Ed Skrein(Ajax), T.J. Miller(Weasel)
Directors: Tim Miller Writers: Rheet Reese and Paul Wernick
Genre: Action & Adventure, Comedy, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 11 February 2016
Length: 108 minutes Rating: R

Synopsis

Based on Marvel Comics’ most unlikely anti-hero, this movie tells the origin of a mercenary Wade Wilson, agrees to participate in a top-secret and illegal experiment after he knew that he has terminal cancer. This illegal experiment left him with uncommon powers such as accelerated healing powers but a disfigured face. These adopts the alter ego Deadpool. With his new powers and a wicked sense of humor, he started to hunt down the person who nearly destroyed his life and countenance.

TOP 2
Captain America Civil Wars

Actors: Chris Evans(Captain America), Robert Downey(Iron Man), Scarlett Johansson(Black Widow), Sebastian Stan(Winter Soldier), Anthony Mackie(Falcon), Don Cheadle(War Machine)
Directors: Anthony Russo and Joe Russo Writers: Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely
Genre: Action & Adventure, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 28 April 2016
Length: 146 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

After the events seen in "Avengers: Age of Ultron" Steve Rogers aka. Captain America faces a new obstacle. As the government seeks to regulate all superhuman activity, two sides emerge; to either side with the Captain, or Iron Man who both have opposing views on the matter. This brings about an epic civil war between the former allies.

TOP 3
Detective Conan: The Darkest Nightmare

Actors: Kappei Yamaguchi(Kudo Shinichi), Minami Takayama(Edogawa Conan), Rikiya Koyama(Mori Kogoro), Wakana Yamazaki(Ran Mori)
Directors: Kobun Shizuno Writers: Gosho Aoyoma
Genre: Animation, Mystery and Thriller Published Date: 7 July 2016
Length: 112 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

A spy infiltrated the Japanese National Police Agency, retrieving secret files of Britain's MI6, Germany's BND and America's CIA and FBI. Rei Furuya and a group of Tokyo Police PSB intercepted the spy during the getaway, and just before the major car accident, FBI Agent Shuichi Akai sniped and crashed the spy's vehicle. The next day, at the aquarium in Tokyo with the Ferris wheel, Conan and the Detective Boys found a woman with heterochromia iris who suffered memory loss and had a broken cell phone. Having decided to stay and help the woman regain her memory, Conan and the Detective Boys are under the watchful eye of Vermouth.

TOP 4
Your Name (Kimi No Nawa)

Actors: Ryunosuke Kamiki(Taki Tachibana), Mone Kamishirashi(Mitsuha Miyamizu)
Directors: Makoto Shinkai Writers: Makoto Shinkai
Genre: Animation, Drama, Romance Published Date: 8 December 2016
Length: 106 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

Your Name (Kimi No Nawa) is saying about the two high school student protagonist, Taki and Mitsuha. One night, their soul suddenly switch place between each other. This strange phenomenon happened randomly. They two must adjust their lives around each other. They communicate by leaving note, writing name on each other face and notebook, then even write about “rules” when using each one body.

TOP 5
Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

Actors: Felicity Jones(Jyn Erso), Diego Luna(Captain Cassian Andor), Mads Mikkelsen(Galen Erso), Ben Mendelsohn(Director Orson Krennic), Donnie Yen(Chirrut Imwe), Alan Tudyk(K-2SO)
Directors: Gareth Edwards Writers: George Lucas, Chris Weitz, Tony Gilroy
Genre: Action & Adventure, Drama, Science Fiction and Fantasy Published Date: 15 December 2016
Length: 133 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

The movie is set before the events in "Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope", during the Age of the Empire. It revolves around a group of resistance fighters who unite to steal the plans to the Death Star, the Galactic Empire`s deep space mobile battle station that is capable of destroying entire planets.

TOP 6
Zootopia

Actors: Ginnifer Goodwin(Judy Hopps),Jason Bateman(Nick Wilde), Idris Elda(Chief Bogo), Jenny Slate(Assistant Mayor Bellwether)
Directors: Bryon Howard, Rich Moore, Jared Bush Writers: Jared Bush, Phil Johnston
Genre: Action & Adventure, Animation, Comedy Published Date: 25 February 2016
Length: 108 minutes Rating: PG

Synopsis

The modern mammal metropolis of Zootopia is a city like no others. This city is a melting pot where animals from herbivores like elephants and shrews to carnivores like tigers and lions can be anyone and allowed to do anything within the permit of law. An uptight rabbit police officer, Judy Hopps, that is determined to prove herself partnered with a fast-talking, wicked fox, Nick Wilde to solve a mystery that involves the city of Zootopia and the conspiracy of the city management.

TOP 7
Doctor Strange

Actors: Benedict Cumberbatch(Stephen Vincent Strange), Tilda Swinton(Ancient One), Rachel McAdams(Christine Palmer), Chiwetel Ejiofor(Baron Mordo),Mads Mikkelsen(Kaecilius)
Directors: Scott Derrickson Writers: Thomas Dean Donnelly, Joshua Oppenheimer, Jon Sphaits, Scott Derrickson, C.Robert Cargill
Genre: Action & Adventure, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 27th October 2016
Length: 130 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

A famous surgeon, Stephen Strange loses his abilities to operate after a serious accident. While looking ways to heal his ability to operate, he found out that one of his patients has fully recovered from his heavy wound with the help of a mysterious sorcerer. He then travelled to Nibel and be accepted by Ancient One. She then introduced him to a new reality and revealed to him that he is the newly designated Sorceror Supreme. He also take on the responsibility on dealing with his former senior, Kaecilius.

TOP 8
Suicide Squad

Actors: Will Smith(Deadshot), Jared Leto(The Joker), Margot Robbie(Harley Quinn), Jai Courtney(Boomerang), Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje(Killer Croc), Jay Hernandez(Diablo)
Directors: David Ayer Writers: David Ayer
Genre: Action & Adventure Published Date: 5 August 2016
Length: 123 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

A secret government agency recruits some of the dangerous super-villains to form a defensive task force after the incident of Batman and Superman. The first mission is to save the world from apocalypse and it will most likely mean the death of them all.

TOP 9
Independence Day: Resurgence

Actors: Liam Hemsworth(Jake Morrison), Jeff Goldblum(David Levinson), Bill Pullman( Thomas J.Whitemore), Maika Monroe(Patricia Whitmore)
Directors: Roland Emmerich Writers: Nicolas Wright, James A.Woods, Dean Devlin, Roland Emmerich, Jamie Vanderbilt, James Vanderblit
Genre: Action & Adventure, Drama, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 24 June 2016
Length: 119 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

People always knew that extraterrestrial beings will be back. Two decades after the freak alien invasion that nearly destroyed mankind a new threat emerges. This alien mothership is twice the size as the last one, the world’s armies must unite to defeat the enemy. Only the ingenuity of a few brave men and women can bring the world back from the brink of extinction.

TOP 10
Batman vs Superman: Dawn of Justice

Actors: Ben Affleck(Batman), Henry Cavil(Superman), Amy Adams(Lois Lane), Gal Gadot(Wonder Woman)
Directors: Zack Snyder, David S. Goyer, Chris Terrio Writers: Zack Snyder, David S. Goyer, Chris Terrio
Genre: Action & Adventure, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 24 March 2016
Length: 151 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

This action-packed film helmed by director Zack Snyder features two big heroes in the lead, namely Batman and Superman. Expect to see a gathering of heroes in this continuation to "Man of Steel".
