TOP 10 BEST MOVIES TO WATCH IN 2016

TOP 1
Batman vs Superman: Dawn of Justice

Actors: Ben Affleck(Batman), Henry Cavil(Superman), Amy Adams(Lois Lane), Gal Gadot(Wonder Woman)
Directors: Zack Snyder, David S. Goyer, Chris Terrio Writers: Zack Snyder, David S. Goyer, Chris Terrio
Genre: Action & Adventure, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 24 March 2016
Length: 151 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

This action-packed film helmed by director Zack Snyder features two big heroes in the lead, namely Batman and Superman. Expect to see a gathering of heroes in this continuation to "Man of Steel"

TOP 2
Captain America Civil Wars

Actors: Chris Evans(Captain America), Robert Downey(Iron Man), Scarlett Johansson(Black Widow), Sebastian Stan(Winter Soldier), Anthony Mackie(Falcon), Don Cheadle(War Machine)
Directors: Anthony Russo and Joe Russo Writers: Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely
Genre: Action & Adventure, Science Fiction & Fantasy Published Date: 28 April 2016
Length: 146 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

After the events seen in "Avengers: Age of Ultron" Steve Rogers aka. Captain America faces a new obstacle. As the government seeks to regulate all superhuman activity, two sides emerge; to either side with the Captain, or Iron Man who both have opposing views on the matter. This brings about an epic civil war between the former allies.

TOP 3
Detective Conan: The Darkest Nightmare

Actors: Kappei Yamaguchi(Kudo Shinichi), Minami Takayama(Edogawa Conan), Rikiya Koyama(Mori Kogoro), Wakana Yamazaki(Ran Mori)
Directors: Kobun Shizuno Writers: Gosho Aoyoma
Genre: Animation, Mystery and Thriller Published Date: 7 July 2016
Length: 112 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

A spy infiltrated the Japanese National Police Agency, retrieving secret files of Britain's MI6, Germany's BND and America's CIA and FBI. Rei Furuya and a group of Tokyo Police PSB intercepted the spy during the getaway, and just before the major car accident, FBI Agent Shuichi Akai sniped and crashed the spy's vehicle. The next day, at the aquarium in Tokyo with the Ferris wheel, Conan and the Detective Boys found a woman with heterochromia iris who suffered memory loss and had a broken cell phone. Having decided to stay and help the woman regain her memory, Conan and the Detective Boys are under the watchful eye of Vermouth.

TOP 4
Your Name (Kimi No Nawa)

Actors: Ryunosuke Kamiki(Taki Tachibana), Mone Kamishirashi(Mitsuha Miyamizu)
Directors: Makoto Shinkai Writers: Makoto Shinkai
Genre: Animation, Drama, Romance Published Date: 8 December 2016
Length: 106 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

Your Name (Kimi No Nawa) is saying about the two high school student protagonist, Taki and Mitsuha. One night, their soul suddenly switch place between each other. This strange phenomenon happened randomly. They two must adjust their lives around each other. They communicate by leaving note, writing name on each other face and notebook, then even write about “rules” when using each one body.

TOP 5
Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

Actors: Felicity Jones(Jyn Erso), Diego Luna(Captain Cassian Andor), Mads Mikkelsen(Galen Erso), Ben Mendelsohn(Director Orson Krennic), Donnie Yen(Chirrut Imwe), Alan Tudyk(K-2SO)
Directors: Gareth Edwards Writers: George Lucas, Chris Weitz, Tony Gilroy
Genre: Action & Adventure, Drama, Science Fiction and Fantasy Published Date: 15 December 2016
Length: 133 minutes Rating: PG 13

Synopsis

The movie is set before the events in "Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope", during the Age of the Empire. It revolves around a group of resistance fighters who unite to steal the plans to the Death Star, the Galactic Empire`s deep space mobile battle station that is capable of destroying entire planets.
