TOP 10 BEST GAMES TO PLAY IN 2016

TOP 1
Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End

Developer: Naughty Dog Publisher: Sony Computer Entertainment
Genre: Action & Adventure Platform: Play Station 4
Player Mode: Single player Published Date: 10 May 2016

Plot

Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End is set three years after Uncharted 3: Drake’s Deception. Nathan Drake, Nathan has retired as a bounty hunter and work at shipping company and married to his wife Elena. One day, Nathan’s brother, Sam Drake come to visit him. Nathan brother seeking help from his brother because his brother threatens by Hector Alcazar, a drug lords and an inmate with Sam. The only way to save Sam is find Captain Henry Avery lost treasure, Libertalia, the pirate utopia deep in the forests of Madagascar.

Gameplay

Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End is an action adventure video game with stealth element play from third-person view. The player control Nathan Drake. Nathan has a good physical body to perform sprint, jump, climb, swim, dive and acrobatic movement to climb obstacle. Nathan can use grappling hook to bypass obstacle. Next, the game feature half open world for the player and provided some vehicle example Jeep and Speedboat for explore the world. Besides that, allies AI also improved, AI will action more effectively when counter enemy and combat status. The player can combine skill with AI allies to perform a combination skill to defeat enemy. After completed the story campaign, the game can unlock special mode by using point player such as bullet time mode.

TOP 2
Overwatch

Developer: Blizzard Entertainment Publisher: Blizzard Entertainment
Genre: Shooting Platform: Play Station 4, Xbox One and PC
Player Mode: Single and multiplayers Published Date: 24 May 2016

Plot

The game is set into the fictionalized version of Earth. Overwatch is an international task force of heroes joins together to restore peace to a war-torn world. The founder of the Overatch is Jack Morrison, Gabriel Reyes, Ana Amari, Reinhardt Wihelm, and Torbjorn Lindholm. After they defeated Omnic Crisis. Overwatch were developed Medical department and Science department, Overwatch recruited many professional that specific in medical and science. After that, Jack Morrison was honored by United Nation for becoming the commander of Overwatch. After a long time, Overwatch was facing internal corruption, expanding military force illegally and sue by people due to violation of human rights. In one day, Overwatch HQ happened civil wars. Jack and Gabriel was die during the civil wars. After that, Overwatch was disbanded by United Nation and release every Overwatch members profile to public.

Gameplay

Overwatch features team-based combat with six players divided into two teams. The player will choose one of the heroes in. with each player selecting one of several pre-defined hero characters with unique movement, attributes, and abilities; There are 24 heroes currently in Overwatch. These heroes are divided into four roles, there are Offense, Defense, Tank and Support (Overwatch, 2016). Overwatch have 4 game mode, there are assault, escort, control and assault/ escort Players will gain Loot Boxes as rewards when gain new levels. Loot Boxes contains character skins, victory poses, emotes and spray. Overwatch have achievements amd trophies to unlock. When the player accomplish the achiements, the player will inlock a commemorative badge of honor and spray.

TOP 3
Gears of Wars 4

Developer: The Coalition Publisher: Microsoft Studio
Genre: Shooting Platform: Xbox One and PC
Player Mode: Single player Published Date: 11 October 2016

Plot

Gears of Wars 4 take place 25 years after Gears of Wars 3, the weather climate change badly due to aftermath. Fossil fuel also finished. Most of the human avoid “Windflare” and migrate to cities with tall study wall. COG invented a robot name Deebees. Deebees function to carry out those dangerous missions to replace human. Deebees can divided into four categories, there are Shepherd, Guardian, Tracker and DR-1. COG announced martial law to prevent any people go outside of wall. If people doesn’t follow COG rules will treat as “Outsider”. The game protagonist is JD Fenix Marcus Fenix son. JD and Delmont decided to leave COG and rely on “Outsider”. One day, a mysterious species, “Swarm” attacked village and catch away Kait mom, Reyna and the whole villager. Without COG assist, JD decided to find his father Marcus Fenix for help and rescue Kait mom and the whole villager.

Gameplay

Gears of Wars 4 is third-person shooter with action with adventure. The game features four different weathers. Thunder storm will be struck the player and the weather can affect the gunshot. Enemy movement and surrounding obstacle will affect by weather. The player can hide and pull enemy backward and eliminate enemy. Next, the game also feature new weapon, close combat moves and knife execution when eliminating enemy.

TOP 4
Battlefield 1

Developer: EA DICE Publisher: Electronic Arts
Genre: Shooting Platform: Play Station 4, Xbox One and PC
Player Mode: Single and multiplayers Published Date: 21 October 2016

Plot

Battlefield 1 come with five story. There are “Though Mud and Blood”, “Friends in High Places”, “Avanti Savoia”, “The Runner” and “Nothing is Written”. Each story mode will control a soldier who are trying to defend and fight for his or her country.

Gameplay

Battlefield 1 is just like previous sequel that highlights cooperation between the player. The game is set in the historical of World War I. The player can use six categories of weapons, there are Shotguns, Sub Machine Gun, Light Machine Gun, Semi Autos, Sidearm and Sniper Rifle. World War I weapon like firearms, grenades, flamethrower in the battlefield. The player can control various armored vehicles. Battlefield 1 multiplayer features 32 vs 32 just like previous sequel Battlefield 4. The game not only allow player drive tank and plane, also features horse. The player can ride horse to hit enemy and damage enemy when riding horse. Multiplayer contain nine game map include singleplayer story campaign map. Battlefield 1 contain six game modes, there are conquest, domination, operations, rush, war pigeons and team deathmatch. Conquest is a large-scale game mode with 64 player control key objective. Next, domination focused combat in specific quarter, the game mode is more focus around strategic objective. Besides that, operation divided into offense and defense, the game mode offer an epic experience based on real battle in World War I .

TOP 5
Titanfall 2

Developer: Respawn Entertainment Publisher: Electronic Arts
Genre: Shooting Platform: Play Station 4, Xbox One and PC
Player Mode: Single and multiplayers Published Date: 28 October 2016/td>

Plot

Titanfall 2 is set in future planet, the protagonist Jack Cooper, a class three rifleman from the Frontier Militia. One day, Jack Cooper was sent to a planet for war but he accidently become a pilot of a Veteran Titan named Bravo Tango BT-7274. BT-7274 is a Vanguard-lass Titan. The titan will obey three protocols to ensure mission complete. Protocol 1 is link to pilot to fight together. Protocol 2 is upholding the mission as long the titan able to fight. Protocol 3 is protecting the pilot at all cost. Player will fight against enemies Frontier Militia and Apex Predator with BT. The war of the game takes in "The Frontier", a region of star systems far removed from the Core Systems. where Earth is located. The IMC seeks to exploit the Frontier's rich resources and try to eliminate Frontier Militia while the Militia fight to protect evasion from IMC and gain independence for the Frontier.

Gameplay

Titanfall 2 is same like its predecessor, eliminate your opponent by using futuristic weapon and summon a giant robot titan into the battlefield and the player can control the titan to eliminate enemy. Titanfall 2 have many different type of titan, example Northstar, Ronin, Scorch, Ion, Legion and Tone (Respawn Entertainment, 2016). For multiplayer game, there are 7 tactical for pilot, there are grappling, pulse blade, holo pilot, stim, cloak, phase shift and A-Wall (Respawn Entertainment, 2016). The game provided many complex map to challenge player agility and response.

TOP 6
Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare

Developer: Infinity Ward Publisher: Activision
Genre: Shooting Platform: Play Station 4 Xbox One and PC
Player Mode: Single and multiplayers Published Date: 4 November 2016

Plot

Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare is set in the future; the earth natural resources have been stripped due to population growth and industrial expansion. Most of the people start migrate to other planet, the nations of the world formed United Nation Space Alliance (UNSA) to support those immigrants. A group of army name Solar Alliance Treaty Organization (SATO) is keeping the peace for UNSA and the immigrant. When UNSA and SATO is celebrating Independence Day, UNSA got a surprise attack from Settlement Defense Front (SDF). The game protagonist known as Lieutenant Nick Reyes, he is the commander of Special Combat Air Recon (SCAR) will fight SDF. He has five allies along the battle. Lieutenant Nora Salter she is the best pilot in SCAR. Ethan, an enhanced tactical humanoid 3rd revision, Ethan can fight at outer space without gas mask, he is more durable than any soldier at outer space. Sergeant Omar, he is commander of a spaceship name Retribution. Admiral Raines, he is Lieutenant Nick Reyes’s mentor since Reyes training at academy .

Gameplay

Call of Duty is a first-person shooter. As the game introduces a new environment of battlefield, outer space with zero-gravity environments. The player is equipped with a boost pack and a grappling hook which allow the player move in the zero-gravity environments easily and take down enemy easily. Infinite Warfare feature futuristic weapon, like laser gun and railgun. Since the game location is set in space, the player can visit different planet in the solar system to eliminate enemy bases at another planet.

TOP 7
Watch Dogs 2

Developer: Ubisoft Publisher: Ubisoft
Genre: Action, Adventure & Shooting Platform: Play Station 4 Xbox One and PC
Player Mode: Single and multiplayers Published Date: 28 November 2016

Plot

The game is set within a fictionalized version of the San Francisco Bay Area, the game is played from a third-person perspective and its world is navigated on-foot or by vehicle. The player play as Marcus Holloway, he is a genius young hacker living in the origin of the tech revolution, the America San Francisco. Marcus will team up with an infamous hacker group name DedSec to take down the city's advanced surveillance system name ctOS 2.0 that used by criminal masterminds to monitor and manipulate citizens.

Gameplay

Watch Dogs 2 is an action-adventure game and stealth element. Watch Dogs 2 is like its predecessor, Watch Dogs, by using the phone to turn the city become a weapon. This time the game let the player create his/her own solution to complete the mission. Next, the player will get a lot more flexibility in how the player hack the enemy. For example, the player can target any person that the player want to hack and can hack with multiple ways and allowed the player to hack most of the electronics and vehicles. The game feature Quadcopter and RC Jumper that printed by 3D Printer. The Quadcopter function is spectating the area situation and drop some bomb on top enemy. The RC Jumper contain robotic arm to interact with any terminal box and reach some place that player cannot reach. There is multiple way to complete the mission. The player can play with full gun blazing to eliminate enemy by using 3D printed weapon. Next, full stealth without detected by enemy. Besides that, through hacking only by using Quadcopter, RC Jumper and take control of electronic to influence enemy. Each successful mission will increase the follower count of DedSec.

TOP 8
Pokemon Sun and Moon

Developer: Game Freak Publisher: Nintendo
Genre: Role-playing Platform: Nintendo 3DS
Player Mode: Single player Published Date: 18 November 2016

Plot

Pokemon Sun and Moon is take place on a tropical island of Alola region just like real life Hawaii. Alola is a peaceful and harmony island. The player moving to one of the Alola region from Kanto region. Professor Kukui requested the player complete the Pokedex. The island has one evil gang named Team Skull. Aether Foundation is an organization use to study about Ultra Beasts. Ultra Beasts is a mysterious creature come from another dimension named Ultra Space (Pokemon, 2016). The Founder of the Anter Foundation, Lusamine seem like have a big conspiracy with Team Skull leader Guzma.

Gameplay

Before the game start, the language menu provided nine language for player to select, there are English, French, Spanish, Italian, German, Japanese, Korean and this is the first time provided Chinese for the game. This generation features Trial Captains to replace previous Gym Leaders. The games also feature some new regional variants. When Pokemon growth in Alola region, they will change form because Alola climate are different with others region. This changes is call Alola form. The game features a new powerful move know as Z-Moves. Z-Moves is a move combine with their trainer to unleash a powerful move to opponent. The way to activate Z- Moves is by equip Z- Ring with Z-Crystal. The game also keep Mega Evolution concept from previous sequel Pokemon X & Y and Pokemon Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire.

TOP 9
Final Fantasy XV

Developer: Square Enix Publisher: Square Enix
Genre: Action & Role-playing Platform: Play Station 4 and Xbox One
Player Mode: Single player Published Date: 29 November 2016

Plot

Final Fantasy XV is takes place on a world with high tech and magic. The mainland divided into three mainlands, there are Lucis, Accordo and Niflheim. Prince Noctis travel with his best friends, there are Gladiolus, Ignis, and Prompto. During the trip to find his fiancée Luna, they have received a news that about his homeland had been invaded and take over by enemy. The news also announces that Noctis and his father has been killed by enemy. Noctis will go back Lucis to revenge from enemy. But first, Noctis need to collect the weapon from the ancient’s king of Lucis to take back Lucis.

Gameplay

Final Fanasty XV is an open world action role-playing games. The player can control Noctis to explore the whole map and explore the open world by using Noctis vehicle named “Regalia” or ridding a lander bird name Chocobos. Final Fantasy XV uses an action based real-time battle system name Active Cross Battle (AXB). During battle, each character has health point (HP) and only magic points (MP) for Noctis. HP will be deducted when take damage from enemy. Magic point will be deducted when Noctis casting magic skill. Noctis can use revival items when HP or MP is less. The player can select, Attack, Magic, skill and Items. Thee game also features Ascension tree. The player can up each character skill in Ascension tree by earning experience, player cannot upgrade ascension tree unless the character is at Inn. Example ofeach character skill is fishing skill for Noctis, cooking skill for Ignis, surviving skill for Gladious and photography skill for Prompto.
